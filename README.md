Umamusume: Pretty Derby Randomizer Manual

This Manual is intended for use with existing Umamusume: Pretty Derby accounts, using whatever Umas and Support Cards you have already obtained on your account and mixing them with everything else.

For best experience when beginning a randomizer of this Manual, it is recommended to make 2-4 “garbage” parents, that is parents that do not train and have low stats and little sparks, to more accurately put the level of your account lower then the progress you have already made on your account.  It is also recommended to only use parents that you have made in careers as you run the randomizer.  Logic is put in such a way that in order to run e.g. dirt races it assumes you have an uma that naturally has dirt aptitude.  This generally only applies to the URA/TS Climax Races.

Starting Rules:

10 random R cards

2 random "1* Beginner Umas"

1 Scenario "URA Scenario"

1 Career Unlock "1* Beginner Uma Careers"

Items are other Umas, 1*, 2*, and 3*s that you included in your yaml options.

Support Cards, R, SR, SSR

Skill Points, calculated to be able to buy skills eventually

Career Wins for 3* Umas, win condition currently win with all chosen 3*s

Locations are all Uma's career goals, all URA Finals race types and then the victory location which is beating all 3* Uma Careers.

Uma career goals logicked by region, having 5 tiers: 1* Beginner, 1* Expert, 2* Beginner, 2* Expert, and 3*.

Each region requires an Uma of that type, also with the unlocked generic career unlock of those tier of Umas.

Yaml options are simply a list of all 3*s currently in EN, with a true/false of including them in generation.  Umas are categorized to have their career wins, their uma item and all their career goal locations to be toggled by these options.

Every Support Card is currently in every iteration of the randomizer.  My logic behind this is that every career run you are required to borrow a Support card so getting an early SSR Support card even if you do not own it is a large boon to your career races by simply looking up someone else that has that card and borrowing it for yourself.  If you do not know about it, there are a couple of sites where you can look up a player that has a Support Card and can friend them to borrow that card.  

[Uma Moe](https://uma.moe/database)

[Chrono Genesis](https://chronogenesis.net/friend_search)

I probably forgot something.
